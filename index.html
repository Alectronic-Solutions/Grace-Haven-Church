About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Grace Haven Church began as a small gathering of believers with a vision to create a welcoming community rooted in faith. Today, we are a vibrant family dedicated to sharing the love of Christ and transforming lives through His grace.

Our Mission

We inspire, equip, and empower individuals to live a life of faith, hope, and love. Through worship, fellowship, and service, we aim to help people grow in their relationship with Christ and make a positive impact in the world.

Our Values

Guided by faith, community, service, and growth, we strive to be a beacon of hope and love in our city. We believe in building meaningful relationships, serving others with compassion, and fostering spiritual growth.

Ministries

At Grace Haven Church, we offer a variety of ministries to help you grow spiritually and serve others. Our ministries include:

  • Youth Ministry: Engaging programs for teens and young adults.
  • Women's Ministry: Support and fellowship for women of all ages.
  • Men's Ministry: Encouragement and accountability for men.
  • Outreach Ministry: Serving our local community and beyond.
  • Worship Ministry: Leading the church in worship through music.
