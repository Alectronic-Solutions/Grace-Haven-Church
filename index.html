Our Story

Founded in Faith, 1995

Grace Haven Church began as a small gathering of believers with a vision to create a welcoming community. Today, we are a vibrant family dedicated to sharing the love of Christ.

We inspire, equip, and empower individuals to live a life of faith. Guided by our core values of community, service, and growth, we strive to be a beacon of hope in Hope City.
